# KYC Document Update

## Definition

Use this category when the customer asks to update KYC, PAN, GST, incorporation, registration, tax, or compliance documents.

## Typical Phrases

- update KYC
- submit PAN
- update GST certificate
- compliance documents
- registration certificate

## Required Intent

The request must involve updating or submitting compliance or identity documents.

## Positive Examples

- Please update our KYC records with the attached PAN and GST certificate.

## Negative Examples

- Do not use for simple address updates unless KYC documents are the core request.
