# Account Opening Status Inquiry

## Definition

Use this category when the customer asks for the status of a business account opening or onboarding application.

## Typical Phrases

- account opening status
- application status
- current account application
- onboarding pending

## Required Intent

The request must ask about the status of account opening.

## Positive Examples

- Please confirm the status of our current account opening application.

## Negative Examples

- Do not use for account closure or statement requests.

## Similar Categories

- CAT-018 KYC Document Update

## Confidence Boost Signals

High confidence when status inquiry appears with account opening or onboarding.

## Evidence Expectations

Evidence should include account opening status wording.
