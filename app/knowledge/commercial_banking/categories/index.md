# Customer Maintenance

* [Registered Email Change](cat_001_registered_email_change.md) - Customer request to update the registered email address used for banking records, alerts, statements, or communication.
* [Registered Phone Number Change](cat_002_registered_phone_number_change.md) - Customer request to update the registered phone, mobile, contact number, or OTP alert number in bank records.
* [Company Address Update](cat_005_company_address_update.md) - Customer request to update registered, mailing, office, branch, or communication address details.
* [Company Name Change](cat_006_company_name_change.md) - Customer request to update bank records after a legal entity, business, or company name change.
* [KYC Document Update](cat_018_kyc_document_update.md) - Customer request to update or submit KYC, PAN, GST, incorporation, registration, tax, or compliance documents.

# Account Maintenance

* [Account Closure Request](cat_007_account_closure_request.md) - Customer request to close, terminate, or deactivate a current or business account.
* [Account Opening Status Inquiry](cat_008_account_opening_status_inquiry.md) - Customer inquiry about the status of a business account opening, onboarding, or application process.
* [Account Statement Request](cat_019_account_statement_request.md) - Customer request for account statements, balance certificates, transaction history, or account ledger copies.

# User and Access Management

* [Authorized Signatory Addition](cat_003_authorized_signatory_addition.md) - Customer request to add a new authorized signatory, signer, mandate holder, or approver to an account.
* [Authorized Signatory Removal](cat_004_authorized_signatory_removal.md) - Customer request to remove or revoke an authorized signatory, signer, mandate holder, or approver from an account.
* [Internet Banking Access Issue](cat_011_internet_banking_access_issue.md) - Customer report of internet banking activation, login, access, or availability problems.
* [Password Reset Request](cat_012_password_reset_request.md) - Customer request to reset a password, PIN, login credential, or security credential.

# Payments and Transfers

* [Transaction Limit Increase](cat_013_transaction_limit_increase.md) - Customer request to increase payment, transfer, transaction, daily, or beneficiary limits.
* [Beneficiary Addition](cat_014_beneficiary_addition.md) - Customer request to add or register a new beneficiary, payee, vendor, or payment recipient.
* [Beneficiary Deletion](cat_015_beneficiary_deletion.md) - Customer request to delete, remove, deactivate, or disable a beneficiary, payee, vendor, or payment recipient.
