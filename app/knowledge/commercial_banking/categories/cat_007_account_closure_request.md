# Account Closure Request

## Definition

Use this category when the customer asks to close, terminate, or deactivate a current or business account.

## Typical Phrases

- close our account
- account closure
- terminate current account
- deactivate business account

## Required Intent

The request must ask to close or terminate an account.

## Positive Examples

- Please close our current account.

## Negative Examples

- Do not use for card blocking or internet banking deactivation only.

## Similar Categories

- [CAT-019 Account Statement Request](/categories/cat_019_account_statement_request.md)

## Confidence Boost Signals

High confidence when close/terminate language appears with account.

## Evidence Expectations

Evidence should include account closure wording.
