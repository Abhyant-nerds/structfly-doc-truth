# Company Name Change

## Definition

Use this category when the customer reports a legal entity or company name change and asks the bank to update records.

## Typical Phrases

- company name changed
- legal name change
- update entity name
- renamed company

## Required Intent

The request must involve changing the customer's company or legal entity name.

## Positive Examples

- Our company name has changed. Please update bank records.

## Negative Examples

- Do not use for address or contact changes.

## Similar Categories

- [CAT-018 KYC Document Update](/categories/cat_018_kyc_document_update.md)

## Confidence Boost Signals

High confidence when legal name, entity name, or company rename appears.

## Evidence Expectations

Evidence should include the company name change request.
